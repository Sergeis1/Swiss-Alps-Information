Bacteria & Archaea

Salmonella — A diverse bacterial group that can occur in soils or waters influenced by wildlife and humans. Good hygiene around campsites is essential.

Crenarchaeota — Cold‑adapted archaea common in alpine soils and glacier forefields in Switzerland; some groups oxidize ammonia and help drive nitrogen cycling (Adamczyk et al., 2019; Pesaro et al., 2002; Zumsteg et al., 2012).
